How to Reset Your Password:
If you are experiencing issues logging in, please follow these steps:
1. Go to the login page.
2. Click 'Forgot Password'.
3. Enter your registered email address.
4. Check your inbox for a password reset link.
5. Click the link and set a new password. Ensure your new password meets complexity requirements (at least 8 characters, one uppercase, one number, one symbol). If issues persist, clear your browser cache or try an incognito window.
